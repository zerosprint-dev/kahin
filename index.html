Signal Language

No "Buy". No "Sell".

KAHIN uses precise decision-support language — never direct action instructions.

constructive
Macro environment supports exposure. Regime and stress indicators are aligned.
watchlist_only
Conditions are mixed. Monitor closely before adding new exposure.
risk_reduced
Stress indicators elevated. Consider reducing position sizing.
avoid_new_position
Regime and stress context unfavourable. Do not initiate new positions.
red_flag_review_required
Critical stress condition detected. Immediate portfolio review recommended.
